Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

At this stage, children explore the world through play-based learning. Our curriculum focuses on early literacy, basic numeracy, social skills, and creativity to develop curiosity and confidence.

Elementary School

Elementary education is designed to nurture critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. We introduce core subjects like Math, Science, Language Arts, and Social Studies while fostering teamwork and confidence.

Middle (10-16) Years
